Stay

Wake up to the beautifully dramatic views of Dubai Marina before heading downstairs for our trademark Mosaic breakfast buffet. Designed to provide our guests with a fresh, contemporary ambience, the apartments are decorated with the finest in linen, technology and accessories.

Our sought-after hotel apartments are spacious, inviting and comfortable, suiting the needs of every type of guest. Offering a selection of deluxe, executive and executive deluxe suites, guests can choose a room that’s perfect for their stay.
