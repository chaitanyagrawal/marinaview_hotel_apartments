catering service

Located in the heart of Dubai Marina, Marina View Hotel Apartments is a luxurious, family-oriented, 4-Star accommodation. Offering a selection of Studio, One-Bedroom and Two-Bedroom beautifully presented serviced apartments, the hotel provides stylish stays for both business and leisure travellers alike.


Hand Crafting Your Affair

Regardless of whether you are celebrating an anniversary, organising a business event or looking for a delectable menu to serve at a home party, Marina View Hotel Apartments offers a large range of catering solutions to meet your every need.

Each of our services is delivered by a highly qualified team of culinary specialists, who pay close attention to every detail, from menu right down to the types of decoration required for your event. Our chef creates some of the tastiest treats in Dubai, choosing from a selection of Continental, Arabian, Russian and Indian cuisine. Onsite, we provide three superb venues to support your event:

  • The Banquet Hall
  • Mosaic Restaurant & Terrace
  • Marina's Pool Sight Restaurant
  • Industrial Catering
  • Private outdoor catering at home or on yachts or dhows

Banquet Hall

Ideal for meetings, events and even private affairs, the Banquet Hall can host up to 80-100 guests at a single time. A private entrance guarantees complete intimacy for you and your party. The prestigious space offers an exciting combination of audio-visual equipment and a team who will assist in producing the perfect experience.

Mosaic Café & Terrace

The newly designed café is a casual yet smart, modern venue with views of Jumeirah Lake Towers. The Terrace allows you to host an open-air get-together during the cool 'winter' months in Dubai, with shaded, air conditioning options in the summer. Warm hospitality, seamless execution and delicious cuisine are the hallmarks of every successful celebration, all of which are available at our hotel's restaurant. You can hire the venue for any type of private gathering and benefit from the expertise of a team of professionals, capable of conducting an event to remember from start to finish.

Poolside Party Hosting

Dubai's warm climate is perfect for enjoying an al fresco experience by the hotel's poolside. The spectacular skyscrapers that form Dubai's iconic skyline encircle the area, providing ultra-modern views of the neighbourhood. Tasty barbecue dishes and scrumptious cuisine of your choice are all freshly prepared by our chefs to ensure that your outdoor event is a success.

Private Parties

If you prefer the privacy of your home to throw an event, or you are celebrating an occasion on board a yacht our dhow, you can rely on our offsite catering to enhance your special occasion. Whether you are organising a wedding, office or birthday party and so forth, our Food & Beverage team will help create a mouth-watering feast full of delightful flavours, which is then delivered directly to your venue.

Industrial Catering

We provide freshly prepared cuisine on a large scale, to match the taste buds of various nationalities from corporate level to labour camps. Our high quality menuis ideal for every ethnic group. This bulk catering facility serves groups of 1,000 and more.
